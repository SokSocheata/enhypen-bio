Enhypen's Albums

ENHYPEN has released an extensive collection of music since their debut, which includes two studio albums, six mini albums, one repackage album, and multiple single albums and digital releases. As of their latest releases in 2024, they have over 70 tracks across various projects. These include main title tracks like "Given-Taken," "Drunk-Dazed," "Tamed-Dashed," and more recent songs such as "Bite Me" and "Sweet Venom." Their latest works are featured on their second studio album, *Romance: Untold*, and their mini album *Orange Blood*, adding depth to their discography with songs in both Korean and Japanese, alongside collaborations and OSTs for series and films.
